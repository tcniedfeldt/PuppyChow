Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

  • - Two Slices of Bread
  • - Peanut Butter
  • - Jelly (or Jam)

Grab a butter knife (with rounded edges). Take one slice of bread and lather peanut butter generously on one side. Take the other piece of bread and spread jelly generously on one side. Flip peanut butter side of one slice of bread onto the jelly side of the other slice of bread. Cut into Batman symbol. Throw against the wall and hope it sticks.
