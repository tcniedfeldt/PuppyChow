Hot Pocket

  • - One Hot Pocket

Open the Hot Pocket package. Put the Hot Pocket on a plate and put the plate in your microwave. Push the "2" button on your microwave for "2-minute Express Cooking". Wait 2 minutes. Wait 1 more minute for the Hot Pocket to cool. Take Hot Pocket out of microwave and shove it down your pie-hole.
