Top Ramen

  • - Package of Top Ramen
  • - Water

Boil water in pot. Open Top Ramen package. Remove spice package from Top Ramen package. Pour ramen noodles into boiling water. Open spice package. Pour spice package into water. By the time you have managed to do that, your noodles are probably done cooking. Done burn yourself as you eat the noodles straight out of the pot (like the animal that you are).
